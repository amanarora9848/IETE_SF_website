About Us

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) founded in 1953 is one of the leading Professional Society in India. With the great vision of founder and subsequent forefathers and stalwarts who were leading light of the Institution IETE, have been devoting and contributing for the advancement of Science and Technology in the fields of Electronics, Communication Engineering, Information Technology and other related subjects.

Objectives and Activities of IETE Students' Forum

  • To plan, organize Technical Programs, Workshops and exhibitions for the benefit of students.
  • To provide common platform for students to exchange of ideas in technical topics of interest.
  • Encourage team spirit and self reliance among student members.

IETE Students' Forum, Thapar University, Patiala indulges in organizing workshops, practice sessions, college-level events, and most-importantly a healthy interaction level among students and seniors in the area of Electrnics and Communication engineering and IoT. The mentors are hardworking, enthusiastic, and ready to help their juniors, always. The students will be taught to be self reliant, and not remain dependent on just the what is being taught to them.
